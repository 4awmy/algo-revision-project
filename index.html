Heap (Array)

  • Parent of i: ⌊(i−1)/2⌋
  • Left child of i: 2i+1
  • Right child of i: 2i+2
  • Leaves: indices ⌊n/2⌋ to n−1
  • Height: ⌊log₂ n⌋
  • Build-Heap: O(n)
  • Max-Heap: parent ≥ children
  • Min-Heap: parent ≤ children
